Italian Brainrot Survive Parkour

By Unblocked Games FreezeNova

Italian Brainrot Parkour Survive follows a simple yet fast paced theme full of wild, meme inspired characters and a demanding parkour obstacle course. The game combines reflex based gameplay with humorous Italian brainrot memes that puts the player’s platforming skills to the test, while they’re either engaging in solo, or offline multiplayer matches with friends. 

Game Overview

Players will be in control of quirky Italian Brainrot characters such as Tung Tung Sahur, Ballerina Capuucina, or Bombardino Crocodilo, each equipped with their own signature moves, animations and voice overs. Your only goal is to survive through an increasingly difficult parkour course, littered with traps, giant snowballs, lava waves, deadly stairs, and much more. 

Controls

Player 1: 

  • WASD - Movement
  • Space Bar - Jump/Double Jump
  • Q: Freeze
  • E: Bomb
  • F: Acceleration

Player 2:

  • Arrow Keys - Movement
  • J - Jump/Double Jump
  • H - Freeze
  • K - Bomb
  • L - Acceleration

Key Features

Unique Abilities

Each player is equipped with their own abilities, such as Tung Tung Sahur using his bat to stun the opponent player. These abilities are automatically activated once another player comes in close proximity, allowing players to keep others away. This element keeps the game interesting, by alerting players to remain vigilant at all times. 

In-game currency

Littered around the obstacle course, players will come across a barrage of coins. Collecting coins is optional, but allows players to unlock new characters. With a total of 11 characters, some are costier than others. Collect as many coins as possible. 

Power-Ups and Boosters

There will be a lot of perks and boosters available for players to utilize. Using these boosters will give players an upper hand since you could paraglide your way across a level, or freeze the players around you, instantly stunning the competition. There are three types of powerups available, Freeze, Bomb, Accelerate, while the boosters include surfboards, paragliders, etc. 

No Checkpoints

There are no checkpoints in between levels, which means players will be required to complete each level in one go, and losing will result in having to completely start over. This keeps the stakes high, and requires players to be extra careful. 

Single and Two Player Mode

There is both single player and two player mode available, allowing players to practice their skills in the solo mode, understand the basic mechanics of the game, and later on compete against their friends in the offline two player mode in a fun battle of platforming fun. 

Tips and Tricks

Double Jump

Time your double jumps right to make the most out of them. They can help players in clearing obstacles that are normally tough to get around, or allow them to cross an obstacle at the last minute. Think of double jump as your second chance to save yourself. 

Boosters and Powerups

Scattered across the playfield you will find boosters that can provide you a shortcut to clearing the levels with ease. According to each level players can utilize them especially within simple obstacle courses. Whereas powerups are especially handy during levels like red light green light, since the acceleration powerup works perfectly with it. 

Coins

It is suggested to collect as many coins as possible in each level to unlock new characters, and pair it up with the 4x boost at the end of each level to rapidly increase your rewards. However, keep coin collection runs separate from simple survival or timed runs, considering accomplishing two things at the same time will pose a survival risk. 

FAQs

How many characters can I unlock?

Players can unlock up to 9 characters through spending coins, while the first two characters come pre unlocked in the game. 

Do different characters have different stats?

No, different characters do not have different stats. The characters solely serve the purpose of customization, with their prices varying depending upon how rare the Italian brainrot character is. 

What happens if I fall?

If a player falls, they would have to restart the entire level, considering there are no checkpoints that would load them back mid level. 

Is there a difficulty setting in the game?

No, there is no difficulty setting in the game, however the levels are designed based upon pre set formats such as red light green light, tsunami parkour, etc. This keeps the difficulty fluid, since depending upon the player’s skillset, one level might be easy for them, while the other might take a few tries to get through.
